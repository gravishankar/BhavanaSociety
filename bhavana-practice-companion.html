A simple companion for continued meditation practice

Let the practice continue at home

This quiet demonstration imagines a simple way to support daily sitting, walking, and mindful reflection after the structure of retreat has fallen away.

This is not meant to replace the living training of the monastery. It is only a humble support for continuity, helping practitioners remember the next small step in the midst of ordinary life.

Practice intention This companion is meant to support sincerity, steadiness, and mindfulness in daily life through simple forms of practice and careful attention to the usual obstacles that arise along the way.

Carrying retreat into the day

A simple rhythm inspired by retreat life, translated gently into home life.

  • Morning: sit before phone, email, or conversation.
  • Midday: pause for a few mindful walking steps.
  • During work: pause mindfully before transitions.
  • Evening: brief reflection and quiet sitting.
